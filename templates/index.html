About Me

I am a printmaking artist based in the Netherlands. My work explores the intersection of traditional printmaking techniques and contemporary themes. I specialize in screen printing, lithography, and etching, creating vibrant and thought-provoking pieces that challenge perceptions and spark conversations.

My work explores various printmaking techniques including screenprinting, etching, and photography.

Within printmaking, I explore how time and speed influence the way we look. In a world where technology evolves at an ever-increasing pace, I seek my position as an artist in relation to that rhythm. My work mainly consists of screen prints, complemented by freehand ink paintings. This results in prints or installations where the viewer is invited to relate slowly to fragments of images and my personal memories. Experience takes center stage, not understanding, but the experience of time itself.
