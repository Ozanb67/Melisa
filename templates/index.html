Iâ€™m a printmaking artist based in Belgium. My practice focuses on how time and speed affect the way we look at the world. In a society driven by acceleration and constant change, I explore where I stand as an artist within that rhythm.

I work primarily with screen printing, often combining it with etching, lithography, freehand ink painting and photography.
